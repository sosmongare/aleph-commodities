ABOUT ALEPH COMMODITIES AND LOGISTICS

Aleph Commodities and Logistics is a diversified conglomerate with operations across Sub-Saharan Africa. Founded in 1985, we have grown into a respected leader in engineering, logistics, supply chain management, and seafood exports.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional services and products with integrity, innovation, and a commitment to sustainable development throughout the African continent.
